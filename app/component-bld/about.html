About

SWIM’s mission is to encourage and celebrate talented females making waves in various aspects of the motion industry by providing a platform for insight, inspiration, and community.

  • Promote Women
  • Provide Inspiration
  • Offer Insight
  • Build Community

Meet the Team

Alicia Reece [ @motiongnome ]

Producer & Co-host

When Alicia isn’t Art Directing at NEIGHBOR (an animation studio she co-founded in Minneapolis), she can often be found wearing fuzzy hats dreaming of tiny houses in Hawaii. Her passion for animation extends from educational content such as her work for TED-Ed “When Molecules Collide”, to experimental media as seen in her live action/2D animation “Western Civilization.”

Julia Iverson[ @_wondertron ]

Art Director & Co-host

Julia is a design, illustration, and animation triple threat whose tenacity shows in her recent promotion to Lead Production Designer at NEIGHBOR. While most girls were playing with their Barbie’s, Julia was self-navigating her way through Photoshop to proficiency. Her skills can be seen in such works as TED-Ed’s “Why Should We Eat Bugs”, which had over a million hits and can now be viewed on Netflix. Her creativity floods into her daily life where she surrounds herself with bright colors, antiques, and an assortment of thrifted treasures.

Amy Schmitt[ @aschmitt6 ]

Producer & Co-host

Amy’s most rewarding role is being a mother to her amazing, strong, kind daughter. Amy currently works as a freelance Art Director, Illustrator, and Animator whose work can be viewed on her website. She enjoys creating experimental animations utilizing texture and patterns in unique and innovative ways. Her love for knitting keeps her cozy in her spare time along with wrapping up in handmade sweaters.

Cortney Rasmussen[ @cortneyPop ]

Web Director & Co-host

Cortney is a woman of the web. She consults as a front-end web developer, and is the web director at GiTMSP (a social network with the purpose to empower women in tech), pretty rad right? She balances her love for technology with a love for the environment, because yes the two actually CAN co-exist. Intertwined with her love for sustainability and the earth is her interest in biomimicry, and her hobbies of backpacking, camping, and fishing.

Lisa Edwards[ lisamarie_eddy ]

Copywriter & Co-host

Lisa is a Producer at NEIGHBOR where she is a self-proclaimed wearer of many hats, a master multi-tasker if you will. When she’s not running around the office like a producer with her head cut off, she decompresses with writing fiction, reading about space exploration, and watching hours of mindless dog videos.
