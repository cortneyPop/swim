Pamela Green

New York native Pamela Green is a Director/Producer in the film and entertainment industry. Her abilities range from creative direction, to producing music videos, to creating main titles for feature films, including The Kingdom, Furious 6, 42, and The Muppets. To say she is multi-faceted is an understatement. On top of being the co-founder of PIC, an entertainment and motion design studio based in LA, she is also fluent in 4 languages. Currently, she is working on Be Natural: The Untold Story of Alice Guy-Blache, a documentary that tells the story of the first female film director and how she was a critical influence on the film industry.
